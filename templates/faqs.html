FAQs - Lemon Quality Detection

What is Lemon Quality Detection?

It's a machine learning-based system that analyzes the visual characteristics of lemons to determine their quality.

How does the system determine quality?

The system uses image processing and a trained machine learning model to classify lemons as fresh, average, or spoiled based on their texture and color.

What kind of images should I upload?

Upload clear images of individual lemons against a plain background, with good lighting and without filters or blur.

Can I use images taken from my phone?

Yes! As long as the image is clear and focused, mobile photos work perfectly.

Is this system accurate?

The accuracy depends on the model training and image clarity. In tests, it achieved around 90% accuracy with high-quality input images.

Who can use this system?

Farmers, vendors, and consumers who want to quickly assess the quality of lemons before buying or selling them.

Will it work with other fruits?

Currently, the model is trained only on lemons. Future versions may support other fruits based on demand.
